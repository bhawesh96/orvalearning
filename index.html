What is Orva all about?

Örva is the medium to embrace the real you, an opportunity to realize your underlying talents and gain the skills to present them. An initiative taken by Srishti Jain, who has been a mentor and counsellor to many, worked in a close contact of the budding generation and has successfully created an impact in their lives.
Örva as a team is a niche venture to provide customized soft skills, for the development of oneself.

If your actions inspire others to dream more, learn more, do more, and become more, you are a leader! Srishti Jain

Srishti Jain

After graduating as a Bachelor of Arts, Srishti took a specialisation in Mass Communication from Osmania University. The mentor didn’t stop here and took a Diploma course in Journalism and Mass Communication followed by a Certificate course in Basic Psychology and Counselling.
After working as a content writer for ‘Indian Express’ and serving as a Picture Research Associate, the mentor joined JNR Pathshala in the administration department and played a key role in overall administration of the school and successfully organized various in-house and open events. After 2 years at JNR Pathshala, the mentor worked as a trainer in speech and drama at All For Kids.
The mentor has successfully coordinated and organized various soft skill program for kids in association with the Indian Leadership Academy.
She has worked as the master trainer at Helen O Grady and the mentor plays a key role in production and provides training in speech and drama.
Srishti has the following qualifications as well -
-> Effective Public Speaking (EPS) from Junior Chamber International (JCI), Hyderabad.
-> Neuro Linguistic Programming (NLP) from Neha Bhatia , Delhi
-> Train The Trainer (TTT) Certificate course from Bodhih institute, Bangalore.
